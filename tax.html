Tax & Regulatory services

Direct Taxation

Tax Planning

Advising clients on structuring their finances to minimize tax liabilities while ensuring compliance with tax laws. This involves strategic planning to optimize tax-saving opportunities within the legal framework.

Tax Compliance

Assisting in preparing and filing tax returns accurately and on time to ensure compliance with tax regulations. They ensure that all necessary documentation and disclosures are in order.

Tax Audit Assistance

Providing support and guidance during tax audits conducted by tax authorities. Chartered accountants help in preparing and presenting documentation and explanations to the authorities.

Representation

Acting as representatives on behalf of clients in dealings with tax authorities. They communicate and negotiate with tax authorities, defending clients' positions and ensuring compliance with tax laws.

Tax Advisory Services

Offering ongoing advice and guidance on complex tax matters. This includes interpreting tax laws, advising on tax implications of business decisions, and identifying tax-saving opportunities or incentives.

Transfer Pricing Consultancy

Advising multinational companies on setting prices for transactions between related entities (e.g., subsidiaries, branches) to comply with tax laws and regulations.

Compliance Review and Due Diligence:

Conducting reviews and due diligence to ensure compliance with tax laws and regulations, especially during mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring.

Tax Dispute Resolution

Assisting clients in resolving tax-related disputes, including appeals and litigation, if necessary.

Employee Taxation Services

Advising on tax implications related to employee compensation, benefits, stock options, etc.

These services are tailored to meet the specific needs of individuals and businesses, ensuring that they meet their tax obligations while minimizing tax liabilities through legal and strategic means.
