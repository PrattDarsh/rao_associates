Tax & Regulatory services

Direct Taxation

Tax Planning

Advising clients on structuring their finances to minimize tax liabilities while ensuring compliance with tax laws. This involves strategic planning to optimize tax-saving opportunities within the legal framework.

Tax Compliance

Assisting in preparing and filing tax returns accurately and on time to ensure compliance with tax regulations. They ensure that all necessary documentation and disclosures are in order.

Tax Audit Assistance

Providing support and guidance during tax audits conducted by tax authorities. Chartered accountants help in preparing and presenting documentation and explanations to the authorities.

Representation

Acting as representatives on behalf of clients in dealings with tax authorities. They communicate and negotiate with tax authorities, defending clients' positions and ensuring compliance with tax laws.

Tax Advisory Services

Offering ongoing advice and guidance on complex tax matters. This includes interpreting tax laws, advising on tax implications of business decisions, and identifying tax-saving opportunities or incentives.

Transfer Pricing Consultancy

Advising multinational companies on setting prices for transactions between related entities (e.g., subsidiaries, branches) to comply with tax laws and regulations.

Compliance Review and Due Diligence:

Conducting reviews and due diligence to ensure compliance with tax laws and regulations, especially during mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring.

Tax Dispute Resolution

Assisting clients in resolving tax-related disputes, including appeals and litigation, if necessary.

Employee Taxation Services

Advising on tax implications related to employee compensation, benefits, stock options, etc.

These services are tailored to meet the specific needs of individuals and businesses, ensuring that they meet their tax obligations while minimizing tax liabilities through legal and strategic means.

We, Rao Associates, leverage our expertise in tax laws and regulations to provide comprehensive and valuable direct tax consultancy services.

Indirect Taxation

We offer various indirect taxation services that primarily revolve around assisting businesses and individuals in complying with indirect taxes imposed by governments. Here are some major services they provide:

Goods and Services Tax (GST) Compliance

Advising and assisting businesses in understanding and complying with GST regulations. This includes GST registration, filing returns, maintaining records, and ensuring adherence to GST rules.

GST Audit Support

Providing support and guidance during GST audits by tax authorities. This involves assisting in the preparation of documents, clarifications, and representation before tax authorities.

Input Tax Credit (ITC) Management

Helping businesses optimize their ITC claims by ensuring proper documentation and compliance with rules governing the eligibility and utilization of input tax credits under GST.

Customs and Excise Duty Advisory

Advising on customs duties, excise duties, and other duties applicable to imports, exports, and domestic manufacturing. This includes assistance with customs clearance, duty calculations, and compliance.

Value-Added Tax (VAT) Consultancy

Even though GST has replaced VAT in many countries, some regions still have VAT systems. Chartered accountants provide advisory services on VAT compliance, registration, and filings.

Customs Compliance and Import/Export Regulations

Assisting businesses in understanding and complying with complex customs regulations related to imports and exports, ensuring smooth trade operations.

Taxation on Special Transactions

Advising on indirect tax implications related to specific transactions, such as inter-state sales, works contracts, leasing, etc., ensuring compliance with applicable laws.

GST and Indirect Taxation Advisory

Providing ongoing advisory services on changes in indirect tax laws, implications of new regulations, and their impact on businesses.

Due Diligence and Reviews

Conducting reviews and due diligence to ensure compliance with indirect tax laws and regulations, especially during mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring.

Representation and Dispute Resolution

Representing clients in discussions, appeals, and litigations related to indirect tax disputes with tax authorities.
